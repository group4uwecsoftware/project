Fun facts about Eau Claire, WI

Did you Know?

10 Fun Facts

1. Eau Claire is French for "clear water," and the city is named after the Eau Claire River that flows through it.

2. Eau Claire is home to the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire, which is known for its strong music program and has produced many successful musicians.

3. Eau Claire is also home to the Chippewa Valley Regional Airport, which offers daily nonstop flights to Minneapolis/St. Paul International Airport.

4. The city hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year, including the Eau Claire Jazz Festival, the Eau Claire Music and Arts Festival, and the Eau Claire Oktoberfest.

5. Eau Claire is located in the Chippewa Valley, a region known for its beautiful natural scenery, including parks, lakes, and hiking trails.

6. The city is home to several breweries, including the popular Leinenkugel's Brewing Company, which was founded in Chippewa Falls, just a few miles north of Eau Claire.

7. Eau Claire is known for its vibrant arts scene, with many galleries, theaters, and other cultural venues located throughout the city.

8. Eau Claire is also home to several major employers, including the Mayo Clinic Health System, the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire, and Menards, a home improvement retailer.

9. The city has been the setting for several movies and TV shows, including the indie hit "Happy Death Day" and the Netflix series "Making a Murderer."

10. Eau Claire has been ranked as one of the best places to live in the United States by various publications, including Livability and Money magazine.
