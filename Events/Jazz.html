Eau Claire Jazz Festival

The Eau Claire Jazz Festival is an annual event held here in Eau Claire, Wisconsin, that celebrates jazz music and culture. It is one of the oldest and most respected jazz festivals in the United States. Each year, the festival attracts thousands of visitors from across the country and around the world.

The Eau Claire Jazz Festival features a wide variety of jazz music, including traditional, contemporary, and experimental styles. The festival showcases both local and national artists, and most of the performances are free and open to the public. In addition to live music, the festival also includes workshops, master classes, and other educational events.

One of the unique features of the Eau Claire Jazz Festival is the Jazz Crawl, which takes place on the Friday night of the festival. During the Jazz Crawl, jazz musicians perform at various venues throughout downtown Eau Claire, giving festival goers the opportunity to experience a wide range of jazz styles in a fun and lively atmosphere.

The Eau Claire Jazz Festival has a long history of promoting jazz education. Each year, the festival partners with local schools and universities to provide educational opportunities for students, including workshops, performances, and mentorship programs. The festival also supports the local music scene by showcasing up and coming artists and providing opportunities for local musicians to perform.

The Eau Claire Jazz Festival has received numerous accolades over the years, including being named one of the top 100 events in North America by the American Bus Association. The festival has also been recognized for its commitment to sustainability, with a focus on reducing waste and promoting eco friendly practices.

The Eau Claire Jazz Festival is a beloved event in the Eau Claire community and beyond. It celebrates the rich history and diversity of jazz music while providing educational opportunities and supporting local artists. Whether you're a lifelong jazz fan or a newcomer to the genre, the Eau Claire Jazz Festival is a must see event.
